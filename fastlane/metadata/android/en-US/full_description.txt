<p><strong>Flux</strong> is a modern and elegant video player designed to transform your local files into an immersive library.</p>
<p><strong>Why choose Flux?</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><p><strong>Organized Library:</strong> Movie, show, and anime information is automatically retrieved via the TMDB service, allowing you to organize your media into a simple and efficient library.</p>
</li>
<li><p><strong>Modern Interface:</strong> Enjoy a cutting-edge design, thanks to an application developed according to Material 3 Expressive principles. Additionally, the app&#39;s colors harmonize with your phone&#39;s theme through Material You dynamic coloring.</p>
</li>
<li><p><strong>Built-in Player:</strong> More than just a beautiful library, you can play your files directly within the application.</p>
</li>
<li><p><strong>Local &amp; Private:</strong> No accounts, no servers, no trackers. Your data and viewing habits stay strictly on your device.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Whether you are an anime fan or a movie buff, Flux breathes new life into your locally stored video collection.</p>
<p><strong>Note:</strong> Flux is an Open Source project developed by a passionate Android enthusiast dedicated to providing a pleasant, simple, and high-performance experience.</p>
