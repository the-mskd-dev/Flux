<p><strong>Flux</strong> is a modern and elegant video player designed to transform your local files into an immersive library.</p>
<p><strong>Why choose Flux?</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Organized Library:</strong> Information for your movies, series, and anime is retrieved via the TMDB service, allowing them to be organized into a simple and efficient library.</li>
<li><strong>A Modern Interface:</strong> Enjoy the combination of Material 3 Expressive and Material You for an application with a modern design.</li>
<li><strong>Integrated Player:</strong> In addition to being a beautiful library, you can play your files directly from within the application.</li>
<li><strong>Totally Free:</strong> No subscriptions, no ads, no trackers. This application is completely free and will remain so.</li>
</ul>
<p>Whether you are an anime fan, a cinephile, or simply need an application to play your files, Flux will make you want to keep watching your movies and series.</p>
<p><strong>Note:</strong> Flux is an Open Source project developed by an Android enthusiast committed to providing a pleasant, simple, and high-performance experience.</p>
